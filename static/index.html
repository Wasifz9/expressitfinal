Translation

Step 1

By completing the form “request for services” below you will initiate your services with Express It and will be in contact with us.

Step 2

We will review your request and contact you to get in possession of your document(s).

Step 3

Once we have reviewed and approved your document(s) for service, we will require 40% of the cost in order to commence translations.

Step 4

Once the full payment is completed, your translated document(s) will be returned to you via email or Dropbox if preferred.

Interpretation

Step 1

Start the process by completing the form “request for service” with all the required information.

Step 2

We will review our calendar and confirm the accommodation of your request or return our earliest available date.

Step 3

Interpretation services have a flat rate of $34.00 for the first hour and $26.00 per every consecutive hour.

Step 4

The interpreter will attend at the set date, time and place to deliver the interpretation services

Who is behind Express It?

Mónica González Prelle

With decades of experience in the trade, Monica Gonzalez has delivered professional services in translations as well as interpretations. Born in Santiago, Chile, Monica focussed on her interpretation and translation career after completing secondary school. Her passion for languages led her to hold positions in international organizations such as the Holiday Inn Crowne Plaza and the Economic Commission for Latin America and the Caribbean both located in Santiago. These interests led her to Canada, where her career further developed in the legal field. Monica has worked as a legal assistant and within her duties she has served as interpreter and translator.
